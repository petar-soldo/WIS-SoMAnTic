Linguistic analysis of posts

It might interest some researchers to see what are the linguistic features of users' posts. We can do this in an automated way, by using a parser. There are many options for this task, but here we will use the UDPipe, "a trainable pipeline for tokenization, tagging, lemmatization and dependency parsing of CoNLL-U files", accessing it through its API. You can test it by copying a post or simply writing a sentence in the text box below.
